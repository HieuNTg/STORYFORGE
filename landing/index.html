3-Layer Pipeline

Story Generation → Drama Simulation → Video Storyboarding. Each layer builds on the last with checkpoint & resume.

13 AI Agents

Autonomous character agents + drama critic, editor-in-chief, pacing analyzer, style checker, and dialogue expert.

Vietnamese-First

Self-Hosted

Quality Scoring

4-dimension LLM-as-judge scoring (coherence, character, drama, writing style) with automated re-enhancement loop.

Rich Export

PDF, EPUB, HTML web reader, and full video storyboards with shot-by-shot camera direction and image prompts.

Smart Routing
