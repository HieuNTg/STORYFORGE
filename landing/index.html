Why StoryForge?

Most AI writing tools produce flat, predictable stories. StoryForge takes a fundamentally different approach.

🎭

3-Layer Pipeline

Story Generation, Drama Simulation, Video Storyboarding. Each layer builds on the last with checkpoint & resume.

🤖

13 AI Agents

Character agents + drama critic, editor-in-chief, pacing analyzer, style checker, dialogue expert, and more.

📊

Quality Scoring

4-dimension LLM-as-judge (coherence, character, drama, style) with automated re-enhancement loop.

🔒

Self-Hosted

Your stories and API keys never leave your infrastructure. Works with any OpenAI-compatible provider.

🇬🇻

Vietnamese-First

20+ Vietnamese fiction genres: Tiên Hiêp, Kiêm Hiêp, Ngôn Tình. English fully supported.

📚

Rich Export

PDF, EPUB, HTML web reader, and full video storyboards with shot-by-shot camera direction.
